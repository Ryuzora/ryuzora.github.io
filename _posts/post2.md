# Upgrading the Battlestation

I finally made the jump from a standard 60Hz display to the Xiaomi G24i 200Hz monitor. 

The difference in competitive games is absolutely night and day. Tracking movements in *Counter-Strike 2* feels completely fluid, and visually confirming frame advantages in *Tekken 8* is noticeably sharper. I have it hooked up to my ASUS TUF F15, running a multi-monitor setup alongside my laptop screen. 

**Initial Calibration:**
* Capped the refresh rate properly in the OS settings.
* Adjusted the color profile to match my primary workflow.
* Verified that the laptop's GPU is pushing the necessary frames without thermal throttling (re-pasting with Arctic MX-7 last month definitely helped).

Next up: refining the Fedora partition to ensure the compositor plays nicely with the high refresh rate.
